INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY DEPARTMENT

Information Technology department is a member of the Computer Society of India (CSI) and ISTE. It offers a four year degree programme in Information Technology with an annual intake of 40 students. The department has a Software Development Lab, Information System Lab, Computer Graphics Lab, UNIX and LINUX Lab, and a Database Management Lab. The unique feature of college is SUN Java Lab, which is not available in any other college of UPTU. In the department, we have elite faculty members with excellent teaching experience. Our institute is affiliated to GBTU, Lucknow and our curriculum has world class norms, in order to provide not only the informational but also to provide and knowledge to create wisdom in this field.
